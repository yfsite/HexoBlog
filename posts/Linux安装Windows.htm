Linux安装Windows

  • 2020-04-15

Tips:原文地址https://moeclub.org/2017/11/19/483/，本文仅提取了文章中安装指令（DHCP）,若需要全文请点击链接查看！

Tips:系统安装CentOS7.6

脚本如下:

win7版:

wget –no-check-certificate -qO InstallNET.sh ‘https://moeclub.org/attachment/LinuxShell/InstallNET.sh’ && bash InstallNET.sh -dd ‘https://moeclub.org/onedrive/IMAGE/Windows/win7emb_x86.tar.gz’
远程登陆账号为: Administrator

远程登陆密码为: Vicer

Windows2012R2版：

wget –no-check-certificate -qO- ‘https://moeclub.org/attachment/LinuxShell/InstallNET.sh’) -dd “https://fr1.teddyvps.com/iso/cn_windows2012r2.gz”

账号administrator

密码Password147

win2008版:
wget –no-check-certificate -qO InstallNET.sh ‘https://moeclub.org/attachment/LinuxShell/InstallNET.sh’ && bash InstallNET.sh -dd ‘http://down.80host.com/iso/dd/WinSrv2008x64-Chinese_datacenter_sp1_vl_kvm_xen.vhd.gz’

账户：Administrator

密码：WinSrv2008x64-Chinese
上面的脚本必须一个标点符号都不能少

执行成功会自己断开连接，然后等20-60分钟就可以了
